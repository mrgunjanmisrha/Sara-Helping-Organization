Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can an ambulance arrive? +

Our average response time in urban areas is 8-12 minutes. In rural areas, it may take slightly longer depending on location and weather conditions.

What should I do while waiting for the ambulance? +

Stay calm, ensure the area is safe for the ambulance to access, and have someone wait outside to guide the paramedics. If possible, gather the patient's medical information and medications.

Do you provide inter-hospital transfers? +

Yes, we provide both emergency and non-emergency transfers between medical facilities with appropriate medical staff and equipment for the patient's condition.

How much does an ambulance service cost? +

Costs vary based on the type of service, distance, and medical care required. Emergency services are often covered by insurance, but we recommend checking with your provider for specific coverage details.
